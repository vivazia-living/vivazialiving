Our Story

Born from purpose.
Built with heart.

Vivazia Living was founded by Andrea Villas-Boas Finkelstein — Executive Director, trilingual interpreter, marketing strategist, empowerment coach, and lifelong learner rooted in the vibrant culture of Brazil and flourishing in Charleston, SC.

"This brand was never about me. It was always about you — the woman ready to rise, the business ready to grow, the space ready to transform."

With over 20 years spanning executive leadership, multilingual communications, HR, operations, marketing, coaching, and training, Vivazia Living exists to serve. To empower. To elevate every life it touches.

We believe that every person holds infinite potential — and that the right guidance, the right space, and the right words can unlock it.
