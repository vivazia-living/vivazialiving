I am Andrea — Executive Director, trilingual interpreter, marketing strategist, and the woman behind Vivazia Living. My journey began in the vibrant culture of Brazil, wound through two decades of professional leadership, and found its home here in Charleston, SC.

"Vivazia represents more than expertise — it is a testament to a shared dream of living fully, beautifully, and intentionally."

With over 20 years spanning executive leadership, multilingual communications, HR, operations, and marketing, I bring a rare breadth of expertise to every engagement.
